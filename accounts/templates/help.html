Help

Setting up your profile

From the dashboard click on Business Profile. From the business profile screen you will see an edit link (image below). This will take you into a form based widget to update the information to reflect your business. If you have two businesses you can switch between them (covered below).

Setting up your profile

The meeting widget on your dashboard shows your next meeting, allows you to link to the zoom meeting, view the agenda, and offer apologies by clicking the apologies link. This only covers groups you are member of.

Attending meetings for groups you are not a member off

Click on the groups directory and you will see all the groups. Clicking on a group will take you into the group profile. All the meetings are listed. By clicking attend next to the meeting you want to attend will cause a request to be generated. Group leads will approve or deny the request (if you are locked out). The status of this is visible on your dashboard.

Meeting Status and Switching Between Groups and businesses

Meeting status will show your requests to join other group meetings. Joining these meetings is currently manual so the host will send you a link. This functionality will be added soon. You can also use the widget to change between businesses and groups

Blogs

You can write blogs on anything you like. Images can be added by adding a url to the image on the internet (screenshot below). Blogs are live when the status is set to publish. You can edit blogs via the blog or edit screen. Previous posts are shown under previous

Profile

You can add a profile image here which shows up in your group info on the dashboard page. You can also reset your password.

Other bits

The market place shows all the businesses in ScreenPop and allows you to browse them. There is a forum which all are free to use, there are topic and gruop forums. There is also a news section to keep ypurself updated.
